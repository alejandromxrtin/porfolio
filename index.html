Who am I?

My name is Alejandro Martín Sánchez, and I am currently studying Fundamentals of Architecture at ETSAM. My academic background, which began with a Science and Technology–oriented Bachillerato, has provided me with a strong analytical foundation and a disciplined approach to problem-solving. These skills align naturally with the methodological and creative demands of architectural education.

A significant part of my development comes from my experience as a 400-meter sprinter in competitive athletics. Training at this level required precision, resilience, and sustained commitment, while balancing sport with intensive studies strengthened my capacity for organization and long-term focus. These qualities now inform my work as an aspiring architect.

I approach architecture with a clear sense of responsibility, attention to detail, and an interest in integrating technical rigor with purposeful design. This portfolio reflects my academic growth and the values that guide me: discipline, ambition, and a continuous commitment to improvement.
